HISTORY

PCJ's Poultry was founded in 2019 by Pravin Jaramogi, an Agricultural Economist and Resource Manager with a passion for sustainable farming. Pravin started with just a small flock of 300 layer chicks and 50 Kenbros chickens, locally known as Improved Kienyeji, on his family farm in Kanyamedha, Kisumu County. Using organic and sustainable methods, he raised the birds to maturity.

MOTIVATION

There were ups and downs along the way, but Pravin managed to overcome them through good agricultural practices. As demand for his high-quality, sustainably-raised eggs grew, Pravin expanded his operation, adding more chickens and building new coops.

ACHIEVEMENTS

Through our extension services, we have helped many aspiring poulterers learn about sustainable farming practices and start their own successful poultry businesses. We are proud to be a part of the vibrant agricultural community in Kisumu County and are dedicated to continuing to provide high-quality products and services to our customers.

Our vision

Our vision is to become a leading provider of sustainable poultry products and services in the region. We aim to achieve this by staying true to our values, building strong relationships with our customers and partners, and continuously innovating to improve our offerings

PARTNERSHIPS

In 2023, he brought on a partner, Collins Omondi Agumba, to help manage the growing business. Together, they began selling live and culled chickens, as well as offering extension services to those interested in learning about sustainable poultry farming. Over the years, PCJ's Poultry has continued to grow and evolve. We now have a team of dedicated employees and a loyal customer base. We remain committed to our founding principles of sustainable farming and providing high-quality poultry products to our customers.

Our Mission

Our mission at PCJ's Poultry is to provide our customers with high-quality poultry products and services that are sustainably and responsibly produced. We strive to ensure that our customers are satisfied and happy with our offerings, and we are committed to continuously improving our practices to meet their needs.
